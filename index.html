Positioning of Light Show Drones in the Sky

The goal of the project can be denoted as follows: how to measure and define the exact positioning of a flying object, e.g. a drone? Many views drones as a serious industrial tool, but they are on high demand in show business too, since they can create amazing visual light shows up in the sky like no other throughout the world. The aim is to understand what different methods could be used to measure individual drone or drone fleet positioning precisely, accurately and dynamically.

Smart Navigation Assistant

Project was part of Huawei hackathon. Participants of the hackathon develop AI applications on Huawei P20 Pro with the use of Huawei AI SDK. Our team won Innovativeness Prix .
The project improves state-of-the-art navigation systems.
To ensure finding a way in complex road and traffic conditions, our application connects the virtual world to the real one. It shows graphical navigation and traffic information in the live picture taken from the phone. In addition, to minimize the chances of collisions with other cars, our application can detect a road lane and distance to the front cars to ensure that customers vehicles are within lane constraints and on safe distance when driving. Moreover, to make sure that traffic is as smooth as possible, we use traffic sign classification. This feature helps drivers to be informed about traffic signs and adjust their driving behaviour to ensure they adhere to road regulations which are currently enforced. Besides that, we implemented drowsiness detection. Driver drowsiness detection is a car safety technology which helps prevent accidents caused by drivers fatigue.
